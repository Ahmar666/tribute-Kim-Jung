Kim Jung Gi

1970-2022

"Where Da Vinci sketched, Kim Jung Gi conjured worlds."

Timeline of Kim Jung Gi:

  • 1975 - Kim Jung Gi is born in Goyang, Gyeonggi Province, South Korea.
  • 1992 - Begins studying fine arts at Dong-Eui University in Busan, South Korea.
  • 1995 - Kim Jung Gi's talent for drawing begins to gain recognition during his time at Dong-Eui University.
  • 2000 - Graduates from Dong-Eui University with a degree in Fine Arts.
  • 2007 - Gains international attention for his live drawing performances, showcasing his remarkable ability to create intricate and detailed drawings entirely from memory.
  • 2011 - Publishes his first art book, "Sketchbook," showcasing his sketches and illustrations.
  • 2013 - Holds his first solo exhibition, "KIM JUNG GI 2007-2013," in Paris, France, further establishing his reputation as an exceptional artist.
  • 2015 - Collaborates with Marvel Comics on a variant cover for the comic book "Wolverine: Old Man Logan" #25, showcasing his versatility as an illustrator.
  • 2016 - Holds his first solo exhibition in the United States, "The Art of Kim Jung Gi," at the Gallery Nucleus in Los Angeles, California.
  • 2018 - Launches the Kim Jung Gi Academy, an online platform offering courses and tutorials on drawing and illustration.
  • 2020 - Continues to inspire artists worldwide through his live drawing sessions and workshops, demonstrating his unparalleled skill and creativity.
  • 2021 - Receives widespread acclaim for his contributions to the art world and continues to inspire aspiring artists with his unparalleled talent and dedication to his craft.
  • 2022 - Kim Jung Gi passes away, leaving behind a legacy of artistic brilliance and innovation. His influence on the world of art and illustration continues to inspire future generations of artists.
